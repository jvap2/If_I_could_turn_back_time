Downloading Geekbench AI for Linux

You are downloading Geekbench AI 1.3.0 for Linux. If your download does not start in the next few seconds click here to manually start the download.

System Requirements

  • Ubuntu 22.04 LTS (64-bit) or later
  • 4GB of RAM

Processor Requirements

  • Intel or AMD processor

Installing Geekbench AI

Once you have finished downloading Geekbench AI, extract the tar.gz file to run Geekbench AI.

Understanding Scores

Geekbench AI scores are calibrated against a baseline score of 1,500 (which is the score of an Intel Core i7-10700). Higher scores are better, with double the score indicating double the performance.

The Geekbench AI Workloads document describes the tests Geekbench AI uses to measure AI performance.

Comparing Scores

Primate Labs publishes the ML Benchmark Chart that enables you to look up the average Geekbench AI scores for other systems.
